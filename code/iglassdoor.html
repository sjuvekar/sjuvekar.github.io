IGlassDoor: An inside look at jobs, companies and salaries, now on iPhone

IGlassDoor brings information from glassdoor.com, the premiere online jobs and salaries community, customized to your iPhone - that means bigger texts, more concise results and better browsing. Now you can

  • Search for jobs in your area and field of specialization. Also search the jobs by job titles you are interested in.
  • See what employers/ex-employers say about a company
  • Browse salary information anonymously posted by glassdoor.com users
  • Take a look at interview process, first hand interview experiences and some interview questions from different companies.
  • Read latest blog posts and tweets from glassdoor.com

Every link in the app takes you to the original glassdoor.com webpage for more details.
