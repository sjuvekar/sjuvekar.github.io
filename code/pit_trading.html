Pit-Trading, A fun trading game

Pit Trading is a fun and informative game, most useful for teaching trading to a complete newbie. Here is how it works in a simulated market:

  • Every trader has a smart-phone with Pit Trading installed on it.
  • The app keeps a list of all market-makers.
  • A trader goes to the market-makers to trade and clicks the row corrresponding to him in the app. This brings up the trading screen as shown here.
  • The trader and market-maker fill the trading information, including price and side on the form. This completes the trade.
  • The app keeps the list of all previous trades executed by a trader and adds them all to find his/her PNL at the end of trading session.
