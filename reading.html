Reading

I am an avid reader and love to read biographies, philosophy and science. Some of my favorite authors include Richard Feynman, Carl Sagan (two of my personal heroes as well!), Paulo Coelho and Malcolm Gladwell. I've listed some of my favorite book here. Most of them are in my collection. I'm trying to get my hands on good hardcover editions of others.

For a more complete and updated list of books I am currently reading or read or reviewed; please visit my weread page here!
